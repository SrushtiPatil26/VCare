FAQs

What is Vcare?

Vcare is the website for doctors and patients. The doctors with different specializations can login to the website. Patient can visit the website and identify their illness

How Vcare handle psychology?

Doctor provide services to people with mental health conditions and those going through difficult or distressing times. People might receive different services from a psychologist, depending on their area of specialty.

Is Vcare psychological clinic?

No. It’s the online platform where patient can book an appointment for their illness and specialist doctor will consult them.

How to talk to psychologist online?

First you have to register on website. Click on book appointment then enter the symptoms which are facing by you. Select the the doctor from list and confirm an appointment with doctor. At the time of confirmation select mode as online or offline.
